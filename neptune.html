Neptune

Radius:

24,622 km

Distance from the Sun:

2,800,000,000 km

How many moons:

14 moons. The four largest moons --- Triton, Proteus, Nereid, and Hippocamp

Atmosphere:

The composition is mostly hydrogen and helium. Trace amounts methane can be found as well. Neptune is also blanketed with the highest winds.

Discovery:

Neptune was not known of since ancient times, William Lassell made detailed observations in 1846.

Fun Facts:

Fact 1: Neptune is the farthest planet from the Sun
Fact 2: Neptune is named after the Roman god of the sea
Fact 3: Neptune is one of the two ice giants in our galaxy
Fact 4: Neptune's orbit is home to Titan, which is one of few moons that is bigger than Mercury
