Saturn

Radius:

58,232 km

Distance from the Sun:

1,400,000,000 km

How many moons:

146 moons. The four largest moons --- Titan, Enceladus, Mimas, and Rhea

Atmosphere:

The composition is mostly hydrogen and helium. Saturn is blanketed with high winds which contain many gases and liquids.

Discovery:

Saturn has been known of since ancient times, Galileo Galilei made detailed observations in 1610.

Fun Facts:

Fact 1: Saturn is one of the only planets in the solar system with visible pronounced rings
Fact 2: Saturn is named after a Roman Titan, the predecessor to the Gods
Fact 3: Some of Saturn's moon have the possibility to support life, as they are home to internal oceans
Fact 4: Most of Saturn's moons is named after titans of the mythology
